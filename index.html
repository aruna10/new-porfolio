About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! I’m excited to have you here. My name is Aruna Gurung, and I’m currently pursuing a Post-Graduate Certificate in Big Data Analytics from Lambton College in Mississauga, Ontario, Canada.

With a professional background in digital marketing, I’m now transitioning into data analytics while continuing to leverage my marketing expertise. My Professional Journey

  • Freelance Content Writer
  • Social Media Marketing
  • SEO Intern
  • SEO Specialist

Freelance Content Writer | Paper Frog Media

During my second year of college, I had the opportunity to write fashion blogs for an outsourcing media house in Nepal. Despite starting with no prior knowledge of fashion or its trends, I embraced the challenge. Over time, as I continued my writing journey, I developed a deeper understanding and became more confident in crafting engaging content.

Social Media Marketing | Meesa Shopping Pvt. Ltd.

In my third year, I interviewed for a position in social media marketing and was selected. Joining a startup allowed me to wear many hats, from graphic design and social media management to content creation, sales, post-scheduling, and customer support. This hands-on experience laid a strong foundation in digital marketing and enabled me to gain a wealth of practical knowledge in the field.

SEO Intern | Prabhavana IT

After a year of working in social media marketing, I wanted to broaden my knowledge of digital marketing. This led me to pursue an internship in SEO during the final semester of my college, where I learned essential SEO practices and strategies. I had the opportunity to work on optimizing travel agency websites, and my content writing skills played a key role when creating SEO-rich content for clients.

SEO Specialist

After completing my internship, I secured a position as an SEO Specialist at one of Nepal's renowned digital marketing agencies. I worked on a variety of niche websites for clients from across the globe. The skills and knowledge I had gained from the beginning of my career became valuable assets in this role. I continued to grow with the company until I moved to Canada for my higher studies.
