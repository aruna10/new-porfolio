Content Writing

My Content Writing Geek!

Welcome to my Content Writing Portfolio! Content writing came to me out of nowhere. I was in my second year of college when I made friends with a few students from another college. Upon conversing with them, I came to know that they were earning a handsome amount of money writing in their free time.

As a teenager with growing needs, I too wanted to earn money of my own while giving equal priority to my studies and college. I no longer wanted to ask for pocket money from my parents so thought that this opportunity was for me a blessing in disguise and I just leaped forward to grab it.

The best part of this job was there were no strict goals or deadlines; you could write it whenever you had time. I did not know much technicality of content writing but I knew I could write pretty well. I had to submit a sample piece of writing before I could really start the job.

I was well aware of the tool Grammarly, I got my content checked grammatically and it sounded all good. But I did terrible! I did not perform keyword research, did not check plagiarism, did not use synonyms, no images, nothing. I still got the job (got lucky somehow).

Then started my content writing journey. I learned from my fellow content writers and in no time I was writing 2-3 blog posts per day. This side gig surrounded the equation: more writing=more earning, but one had to go through a quality check, deliver the best piece of content one could, and finally approval from the company side.
